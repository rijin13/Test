3DTiler

The 3DTiler is a WebGIS-App to georeference 3D building models and create 3D Tiles tilesets. It was developed for two research projects of the University of Cologne and is part of a Bachelor thesis. In its current state the 3DTiler is highly customized to the requirements and data of the projects and therefore not universally usable.
